Water Heaters

We install and service all types of water heaters. Whether your house is old or new, uses gas or electricity, or if you’re wanting to update your water heating system, we are here to help.


There are many options and sizes available when it comes to choosing water heaters. We proudly install water heaters by Bradford White and Rinnai.

Get Endless Hot Water with Rinnai Tankless Water Heaters

Tankless heaters deliver endless hot water and reduce energy costs. Gibson Plumbing is proud to carry tankless water heaters by Rinnai, the brand leader as chosen by home builders in Builder Magazine and recognized by The Alliance to Save Energy for energy efficiency. Rinnai water heaters come with a 10-year manufacturer’s warranty. We carry the following models:
