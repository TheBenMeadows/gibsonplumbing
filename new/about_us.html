About Us

Gibson Plumbing is a full service residential and commercial plumbing company serving San Antonio, Austin, Dallas and surrounding areas. In business since 1954, our licensed plumbers provide solutions from large multi-family projects to single-family residential. We provide free estimates and consultations to determine the ideal solution for your plumbing needs.

Our Plumbing History

1954 - Upon returning from serving overseas in the military, William C. Gibson worked for several contractors in the San Antonio area, earning his Master Plumbers License. Working during the day on new sites, he spent his evenings estimating jobs, and in 1954 Gibson Plumbing Company began with two trucks.

1972 - Mr. Gibson was joined in the business by his son William A. "Billy" Gibson. Together they continued the growth of the company having earned the trust and respect of Home Builders and Home Owners not only in San Antonio, but nationwide. The Gibsons contributed their success to the basic values of quality workmanship, exceptional customer service and fair pricing. These values are still the basis of the company today.

2003 - Preparing for retirement the Gibsons decided it was time to sell the company. In assisting the Gibsons in the sale, Barry Bankler became very familiar with the company, its history and its basis for success, and decided that he wanted to carry on the company's traditions, so he bought Gibson Plumbing.
