Careers

Gibson Plumbing is an innovative company. We treat our employees with fairness and respect, in addition to providing the best pay and benefits. We are looking for people with the right stuff to help us build tomorrow’s communities.

We are currently offering both Hourly Rate and Piece Rate Tech Positions.

 

All applicants must:

  • Be at least 17 years of age
  • Be drug-free
  • Have mechanical ability
  • Have good math skills
  • Enjoy being outdoors
  • Have valid driver’s license
  • Have a strong work ethic

 

We offer our employees:

  • Health and dental coverage
  • Paid vacations
  • Piece-rate pay
  • Hourly pay
  • Bonuses
  • Tool program
  • Opportunities for advancement

If you are a motivated individual and are looking for a challenging and rewarding career in the construction industry, please email us your resume or request an application.
